ROLES IN WALMART BUSINESS OPERATIONS (Stores)

There are different teams that play a vital part in getting an item to the store. The different process that happens are : Strategy formulation , Supplier identification and negotiation, product development, assortment finalization, line reviews, management walk through and approvals, item creation, modular creation, replenishment and checking in items to the respective stores.

The main actors involved in the above process are :

  • Buyer
  • Business Analytics (Planner)
  • Sourcing
  • Product Development
  • Replenishment
  • Modular
  • Order Specialist
