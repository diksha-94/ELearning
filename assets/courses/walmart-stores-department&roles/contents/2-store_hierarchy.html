STORE HIERARCHY

Walmart store is organized in a particular hierarchy for financial reporting. Segment is the highest level followed by SBU(Strategic Business Unit). Store has different SBUs like Apparel, Entertainment, Hardlines, Consumables, Food, Health &Wellness, Toys & Seasonal and Home.
