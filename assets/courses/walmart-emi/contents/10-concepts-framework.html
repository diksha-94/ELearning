EMI Concept Framework

  • One of the important EMI features is the introduction of the following new concept levels in the item file for classifying item information:
    • Core Product
    • Product
    • Offering
    • Trade Item
    • Supply Item.

  • This concept level approach will reduce the data redundancy across different interfacing applications. The concepts will allow business processes to operate at different levels of information as they need it.

  • Will bring control to item information hence minimizing data inconsistencies across applications.

  • These four concepts distinguish the customer facing attributes of the product from more lower level supply chain related attributes which are more supplier/manufacturer facing.

  1. Customer facing - Core Product , Product and Offering Concept
  2. Supplier facing - Trade and Supply Item Concept
