EMI- Business Benefits

Single, Unified Customer Facing Item File for the Enterprise
  • Common product item identifier spanning all lines of business and formats
  • Availability of rich content for all products
  • Introduction of customer facing product concepts (Core Product, Product, Offering)
  • Relationship between product and supply chain item data
Streamlined Item Setup Process
  • Common item setup process for brick and mortar stores and online
  • Customer attributes and business process governance
  • Emphasis on industry standard data and reduction of manual data entry during item creation
  • Availability of master item data for a common merchandising workflow including assortment planning, compliance, pricing, and sourcing
Customer-Driven Attribute Governance
  • Reduced Purchase Order (PO) corrections/ revisions
  • Improved data quality and consistency
  • Visibility across business segments
  • Better enterprise level decision making
  • Foundation for strategic physical to digital initiative of Walmart
  • Concept level approach to item management to enhance data control and governance
  • Business Automation
  • Sunset legacy systems
