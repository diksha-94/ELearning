Walmart & EMI Introduction

  • Course Objective

After the completion of this course, the user should be able to:

  • Get a background of Walmart Inc and its operating divisions
  • Get a high level overview of the Global eCommerce Operations
  • Understand the fundamentals of Enterprise Master Item
  • Learn the high level business benefits of EMI
  • EMI's key components
  • The 4 Concepts introduced by EMI - Product, Offer, Trade Item, Supply Item

Following Topics are covered in this course:

  • Walmart / Walmart US
  • Walmart International & Sam's Club
  • Walmart Global eCommerce (GeC)
  • Enterprise Master Item (EMI)
  • EMI- Business Benefits & Stakeholders
  • Work flow : Current state vs Future state (EMI)
  • Key components of EMI
  • EMI Concept Framework
