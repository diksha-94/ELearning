Walmart & EMI Introduction - Glossary

Term Definition
GeC Global eCommerce - refers to the Walmart Online operations
EMI Enterprise Master Item - The unified item management application
PO Purchase Order - Gives the final list of items that would be ordered from the supplier
Trade Item Refers to any item (product or service) upon which there is a need to retrieve pre-defined information and that may be priced or ordered or invoiced at any point in any supply chain
Supply Item A Supply Item is a Walmart specific view of goods or services intended for the sale to the customer.
Offer It holds information about the way a customer will purchase the product and how much they will be paying for it.
Store Hierarchy The financial hierarchy which is used to classify all the products sold in Walmart Stores
SBU Strategic Business unit which is the highest level in the hierarchy for all similar products. All the financials for these items roll up to the SBU
Walmart SPEC Walmart Enterprise specification will be the external facing document to collect product, offer, supply and trade item level attributes required to set up an item on Walmart.com
