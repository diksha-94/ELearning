Key Components - Contd.

Pre Item

Provides the ability to capture minimal data to generate any level of Item concepts (product, offer, supply/trade item)

Helps the buying, replenishment, modular, pricing, bid and BLR processes to flow seamlessly from concept to creation.
  • Ability to view product concepts prior to definition of product approvals
  • Ability to generate draft concepts with minimal data
  • Ability to generate future maintenance records
Attributes

This is for capturing valid, product specific details for each item based on the category/subcategory it belongs to.

  • Provides ability for efficient capture of product data and images
  • The Item attributes are business managed
  • Governance process is in place to ensure only the valid attributes are made visible to the supplier
Quality
  • Quality of the Item data is ensured by determining prompt and actionable tasks for each role
  • All correction of errors will now be pushed back to the supplier
  • Supplier Scorecard and Ranking will keep suppliers on their toes
