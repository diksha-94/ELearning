Walmart US

Walmart US is the company's largest operating division. Walmart US can be classified as
  • Walmart Supercenter
  • Walmart Discount Store
  • Walmart Neighborhood Market

Walmart Supercenter

  • Supercenters are hypermarkets with sizes varying from 69,000 to 260,000 square feet.
  • Offers a one-stop shopping experience for electronics, apparel, toys and home furnishings with the added convenience of a grocery store with fresh produce, bakery, deli, meat and dairy products.
  • Most Supercenters are open 24/7 and may also include specialty shops such as pharmacies, limited financial services like bill payment, banks, hair and nail salons, name-brand restaurants, vision centers or health clinics

Walmart Discount Store

  • Discount stores with the average store size of about 104,000 square feet are smaller than Supercenters.
  • They carry general merchandise and limited groceries.

Walmart Neighborhood Market

  • Walmart Neighborhood Market is a chain of smaller-footprint grocery stores for communities in need of a pharmacy, affordable groceries and merchandise (goods to be bought and sold). Each one is approximately 38,000 square feet and employs up to 95 associates.
  • They are used to fill the gap between supercenters and discount stores, offering a variety of products including full lines of groceries, pharmaceuticals, health and beauty aids, photo developing services and a limited selection of general merchandise.
