Supply Item

  • A Supply Item is a Walmart specific view of goods or services intended for the sale to the customer.
  • It was designed to meet the various internal needs of the supply chain and the product's alignment to different business segments like stores, Sam's and dotcom.
  • The Supply Item contains required information regarding the product, supplier, the replenishment channel, pack configurations and the associated cost.
  • For any given product there could be multiple Supply Items in Walmart (based on the supplier, replenishment method, cost and pack configurations).
  • The Supply Item concept has been used to support multiple Business areas (including Sourcing, Assortment and Modular Management, Pricing, Logistics) in both Walmart and Sam's Club for many years.
  • Items in Walmart today are maintained at the Supply Item
