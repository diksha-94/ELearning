Offer

  • The Offering consists of the Product, the Seller and the Walmart legal entity through which a Product is offered for sale.
  • It holds information about the way a customer will purchase the product and how much they will be paying for it.
  • The region of business operation, tax structure, consumer buying pattern will influence the offer that the legal Walmart entity gives its customers.

Benefits of Offer concept

  • Pricing -Ability to set different prices at the corporate, online and Store/Club level as per business need.
  • Taxes - Currently, Taxes reside at the Supply Item level for a given Product; Changing it to the Offer level would make it easier to manage.
  • Compliance - Managing compliance across markets and business segments will be easier at this level.
  • Marketing - Product marketing can be driven to specific Walmart entities and markets
