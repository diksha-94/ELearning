Walmart Global eCommerce (GeC)

  • Walmart GeC or Dotcom is one of the world's largest, fastest-growing and most dynamic eCommerce organization based on California's Silicon Valley, with operations in Bangalore, India and Sao Paulo, Brazil.

  • Walmart's Global eCommerce division provides online retailing for Walmart, Sam's Club, ASDA and all other international brands. It provides a seamless experience that lets customers shop anytime and anywhere using online, mobile, social and stores. Walmart.com, founded in 2000, currently sees 45 million visits a month and is growing every year.

  • @WalmartLabs is the technology group in GeC and is an innovation hub in areas like Mobile & Social Shopping, Search & Personalization and Platform to market. @Labs is constantly focusing on improving search, enhancing pricing and optimizing marketing to provide a wide assortment of products at lowest prices, when and where customers want it.

  • Mobile empowers customers and helps connect online and offline channels to improve customer experience by innovating convenient ways to shop both online and Instore.
