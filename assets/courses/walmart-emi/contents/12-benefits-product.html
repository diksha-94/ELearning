Benefits of Product concept

  • Webpage management - Most webpages are centered around products
  • Image management - Each product would require at least one image, hence images will be managed at this level
  • Space management and modular - For stores, space analytics and modular planning is based on the product
  • Customer analytics - Sales and customer buying behavior can be analyzed at the Product level
  • Marketing - Marketing materials directly refer to products
  • Sourcing - Buyers can specify the product variations they intend to purchase from specific sourcing initiatives
