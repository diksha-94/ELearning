Trade Item

  • A Trade Item represents the supplier or manufacturer view of how the product will be priced, ordered, or invoiced in the supply chain.
  • All the different ways a supplier can package the product and send it to the retailer are captured in the Trade Item level.
  • Trade Item has a hierarchical structure as outlined below.
  • Each level of the above hierarchy is a separate trade item identified by a GTIN (Global Trade Identification Number) and has different information like dimensions, weight, quantity, handling procedures, etc.

Benefits of Trade Item concept

  • Order Management - All orderable product information is included in the trade item for a given Supplier
  • Inventory management - Inventories can be ordered at this level which will help in forecasting and overall inventory management
  • Information Sharing - Information is published by the supplier to Walmart via GDSN at this level
