GS1

GS1 is a neutral, not-for-profit, international organization that develops and maintains standards for supply and demand chains across multiple sectors.

The whole supply chain works more efficiently when everyone follows the same business rules and speaks the same supply chain language. These rules and language are known as the GS1 System of Standards.

Most companies initially come to GS1 to get a bar code number for their products. GS1 US is the GS1 Member Organization in the US. GS1 US is responsible for managing the GS1 System in the USA.

GS1 US assigns GS1 Company Prefixes to companies/organizations in the USA. A GS1 Company Prefix is a unique identification number that GS1 US licenses to a company - and it is part of every barcode and identification number the company creates.

DSV

DSV stands for Direct ship Vendor where the orders are directly shipped to Customer. The DSV items are only created for sale online. These are not stored in Walmart DC's or Stores. The item type for DSV is 07. The supplier ships the items directly to the customer but Walmart still owns the inventory and handles any return process.
