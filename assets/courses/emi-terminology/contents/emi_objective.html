EMI - Terminologies

  • Course Objective

After completion of this course ,the user should be able to :

  • Understand the Key terminologies used as part of EMI system like GS1, DSV, GDSN, UPC,GTIN and GLN
  • Understand different Item types used in Walmart
  • Learn the concept of supplier/vendor number
  • Get to know the concept of Item pack quantity and Item status definitions
  • Understand the concept of assortments and shippers

Following topics are covered in this course:

  • Key terminologies used as part of EMI system
  • Concept of Item type, supplier number, Item pack quantity and Item status definitions
  • Concept of assortments and shippers.
