WALMART ITEM NUMBER

  • Every merchandise item in Walmart is identified by a unique number - Walmart item number. It can vary between 6 to 9 digits.
  • Every piece of merchandise that goes into a Wal-Mart store has a UPC label attached to it which is assigned from each supplier.
  • To enable the merchandise to scan in a store, we create a Wal-Mart item number in our system and attach it to the UPC.
  • This Wal-Mart item number is what we use to write purchase orders, receive the merchandise into the stores, and track sales and on-hands.
