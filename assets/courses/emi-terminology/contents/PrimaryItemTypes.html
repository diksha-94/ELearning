Item Type (Cont'd)

  • DSD ( Direct to Store Delivery)

    : This type of item is brought into the stores by the suppliers. There is not a systematically generated purchase order in host. The supplier will determine what is needed and bring it in on a store generated PO. This item type is Non Replenishable.

  • Non Replenishable

    : These are items used by the buyer to write promotional orders. Although we have this specific item type to ship promotions against, domestic suppliers may ship a non-replenishable or replenishable item type as a promotion through a distribution center or directly to the store as determined on the PO type.
