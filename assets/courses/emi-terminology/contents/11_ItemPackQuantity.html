Item Pack Quantity

  • Vendor pack quantity is the total amount of units in the box that ships from the supplier.
  • Warehouse pack quantity is the total amount of units in the inner pack shipped to store.
  • Case Pack Items - Do not contain inner packs. The quantity the supplier ships to the DC is the quantity shipped to the stores. The item will be built with the total quantity as the vendor pack and the warehouse pack.
  • Break Pack Items - The vendor pack contains inner packs. The vendor will ship the inner packs contained within an additional box. The DC will break open the vendor pack and ship the warehouse packs to the stores. Store distributions are created using warehouse packs.
