Item Number and Type

Every piece of merchandise that goes into a Wal-Mart store has a UPC label attached to it which is assigned from each supplier. To enable the merchandise to scan in a store, a Wal-Mart item number is created in the Wal-Mart system and attached to the UPC.

This Wal-Mart item number is used to write purchase orders, receive the merchandise into the stores, and track sales and on-hands. Wal-Mart Item number can vary between 6 to 9 digits.

All item numbers are assigned an "item type" which will determine how the product will get to the store.

There are 4 primary Item Types are :
  1. DSD (Direct to Stores Delivery)
  2. Non Replenishable
  3. Assembly Replenishable
  4. Warehouse Replenishable
