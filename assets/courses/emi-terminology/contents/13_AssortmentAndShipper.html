Assortment and Shipper

Assortments and shippers allow multiple items with different UPC codes to be grouped together for shipping and/or ordering purposes. This provides a way to order and ship the right combination and quantity of merchandise to the stores based on customer preferences & seasons.

Assortment Shipper
  • Commonly called "like items" because all of the items are of the same except for a detail such as color
  • Have the same cost, retail and pack
  • Parent Item Replenishable
  • Sales Reporting on Child Items
  • Commonly called "unlike items" because the contents can
    either be completely different items combined in a box or similar items of definite quantities of each
  • Have different cost, retail and pack
  • Non-Replenishable Only
  • Sales Reporting on Individual Items
