UPC

The UPC is the barcode representation of the GTIN 12 which consist of 12 numeric characters which can uniquely identity a company's individual product. The main purpose of UPC is used for scanning of trade items at the point of sales.

Data structure of UPC:

The first either 6 to 9 nine digit in a UPC is referred as company prefix assigned by the GS1 and this uniquely identifies the company and this remain constant as per the GS1 standards.

The next set of digit is the product number and uniquely identifies the individual items. The last digit is check digit based on the mathematical calculation of the first 11 digit.
