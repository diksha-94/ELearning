Supplier Number/ Vendor Number

When suppliers want to sell their products to Wal-Mart, they are required to fill out a legal document called "supplier agreement". This document is sent to the Vendor Master team to assign a six digit Supplier number.

Every item in the Wal-mart system must have a 9-digit supplier number attached.

Composition of a 9-digit supplier number:
  • Supplier Number - The 6-digit number assigned to the supplier by Vendor Master.
  • Department Number - The 2-digit department number that the item will be sold in.
  • Sequence Number - The last digit refers back to the supplier agreement.
