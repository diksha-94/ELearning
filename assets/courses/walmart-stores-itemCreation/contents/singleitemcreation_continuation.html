Entering Item Attributes:

  • Required Fields - All fields highlighted in Green are required to create the item. These fields will differ by item type and line of business (softlines, hardlines, and grocery).

Completing the process:

  • Save - Item information may also be entered and held before allowing the item to create by clicking the Save button. This will put the item information in the task page list for later completion.
  • Create - Click on the Create button at the bottom of the screen to generate an item number.
  • Error Notification - After clicking the Create button, the system will validate all the information entered. Any missing required information or any information entered incorrectly will be highlighted in Red. If the cursor is hovered over that field it will show the details of error.
