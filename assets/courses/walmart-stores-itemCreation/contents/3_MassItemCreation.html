MASS ITEM CREATION

Item Creation Mass entry enables creating multiple item numbers at the same time.

Getting the Item Creation Spreadsheet Template:

When choosing to create more than one item, the Item Creation Spreadsheet Template should be used. This link is located at the top of the Create Items Section under the Task Bar.

Suppliers Complete the Item Creation Spreadsheet

  • Suppliers have access to this on Retail Link. They can fill it out and then e-mail to the OS that the information is complete in RL.
  • Once the spreadsheet is completed and proofed by the Buyer, planner and RM, the OS will save it.

For Supplier Quote and Data Sync, the process is the same from here on. The Item Creation Spreadsheet is downloaded from the respective tabs.

Items are created by uploading the spreadsheet in Retail Link\ OIF\Create Item\Import completed Item Creation Spreadsheet.
