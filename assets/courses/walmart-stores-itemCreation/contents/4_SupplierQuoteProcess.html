SUPPLIER QUOTE PROCESS

(Primarily used for Import Items)

Pre Item Creation : Supplier Quote (via System)

If a Quote has been complete and already staged (system):
  • Click on OIF line "View Quote Items Pending Creation".
  • Select the DEPARTMENT and BUYER. Or enter the QUOTE ID
  • Click on View Quotes
  • The quote(s) will be listed on the next screen with the number of items and descriptions.
  • Click on the "Download Item Creation Spreadsheet" button to generate item creation template (which is proofed and approved by the Buyer, RM and Business Analytics).
