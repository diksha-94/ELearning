ITEM CREATION

Once the proposed assortments are reviewed and approved by the Leadership Team for any Buy Season, Buyer communicates the "committed list" to Cross Functional Teams as well as to Suppliers.

Import item information are captured through Supplier Quote while the domestic items information are published through GDSN (Global Data Synchronization Network) to Data Synch.

Order Specialists download Form 33 (Item Creation Sheet) and requests Replenishment Manager to review the item information against approved Line Reviews.

Once Replenishment Manager reviews and approves Form 33, Order Specialists upload the approved Form 33 into Online Item File (OIF) to perform OIF validation.

Once the OIF validation is passed through, WM Item Number is successfully generated. Order Specialists work with RM / Buyer and Supplier (if needed) for any OIF validation errors.

Two types of Item Creation Processes are:

  1. Single Item Creation
  2. Mass Item Creation
OIF can be accessed through Retail Link(RL) using the below said link: https://rlif2k.wal-mart.com/itemfile_us_wm/servelet/home
