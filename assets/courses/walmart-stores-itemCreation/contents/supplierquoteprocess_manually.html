SUPPLIER QUOTE PROCESS

Pre Item Creation : Supplier Quote (Manually)

If a quote has been complete, but has not been staged, or connected to OIF for Item Creation, the quote will need to be staged. To stage a quote:
  • Click on Stage quote items from "Supplier Quote"
  • Enter Quote ID
  • Click Enter
  • Click on Create Items in OIF
  • This completes the staging process.
  • Click on the quote ID to see the items that can be created.
  • Click on Download Item Creation Spreadsheet (Form 33 - which is proofed and approved by the buyer, RM and planner).
