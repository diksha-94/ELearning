Assortment and Shipper - Glossary

Term Definition
Like Assortment All items in the assortment are same except for details like color, size, etc.
Unlike Assortment Contents in the assortment can be completely different or similar
Parent Item Number The item number that represents the item (box) in which like assortments are grouped
Prime Item Number The item number that represents the item (box) for a Shipper
Child Item Number Item number that represents the individual items inside an Assortment box
Content Item Number Item number that represents the individual items inside a Shipper box
Supplier Pack Quantity Quantity of items that ship from the Supplier to the Warehouse
Warehouse Pack Quantity Quantity of items that ship from the Warehouse to the Stores
Break Pack When Supplier Pack Quantity > Warehouse Pack Quantity, the warehouse breaks down the pack shipped by Supplier and ships separate packs to the stores
Case Pack When Supplier Pack Quantity = Warehouse Pack Quantity. All that the Supplier ships is sent to one store
On Hands Quantity of items available and tracked at DC & Store level
On Orders Quantity of items ordered and tracked at DC & Store level
PDQ Presentation Display Quantity
