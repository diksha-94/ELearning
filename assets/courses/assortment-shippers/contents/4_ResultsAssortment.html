Results of using an Assortment

Store Level Ordering

  • Stores can only order and/or receive an entire assortment.
  • If the store scans any child UPC within the assortment to place an order, the entire assortment will be ordered.
  • Sales and on-hands are seen on the assortment parent which drives replenishment for the assortment.
  • Sales post on the child item numbers but roll to the assortment parent item number.
  • On-hands and rate of sale will post on the prime item number. If any item is viewed at store level it will show the entire assortment on-hand and rate of sale.

Home Office (Corporate) Reporting

  • Sales are seen on each child item number.
  • On-hands are seen on the assortment parent.
