Assortment & Shippers

  • Course Objective

After the completion of this course, the user should be able to:

  • Understand the Assortment & Shipper concepts followed in WM
  • Understand the criteria for creating Assortment & Shipper
  • Explain the difference between Assortment and Shippers
  • List down the step by step procedure to create Assortments & Shippers in EMI

Following topics are covered in this course:

  • Assortment and Shipper Concepts
  • Assortment and Shipper creation in EMI
