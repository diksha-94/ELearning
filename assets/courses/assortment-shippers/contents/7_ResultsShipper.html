Results of using a Shipper

Store Level Ordering

  • Sales and on-hands are seen on each content item number.
  • The prime item number is only used to write the purchase order, thus ship the merchandise to the stores in one carton.
  • The prime does not hold any sales or on-hand information from the content items.
  • The receiving quantity per content item number will post to the content item within the shipper.

Home Office (Corporate) Reporting

  • Sales and on-hands are posted on each content item number.
  • The prime item number is only used to write the purchase order.
