Assortments and shippers consist of two parts, but these are called by different names depending on which shipping method is being referred to

  • One item is created to use as the assortment 'parent' or a shipper 'prime'. This item is used to represent the entire assortment or shipper on purchase orders. These 'parent' or 'prime' items do not sell at the register and can be thought of as the box in which the selling units are shipped.

  • Other items are created and linked to the assortment or shipper item numbers. These items represent each selling UPC that will be included in the assortment or shipper. These inner units are referred to as assortment 'child items' or shipper 'content items'.
