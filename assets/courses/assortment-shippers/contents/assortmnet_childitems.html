Assortment - Best Practices (Cont'd)

Assortment Child Items

Below are some factors to be considered while creating the assortment child item numbers:
  • Replenishable item numbers do not exist under the same UPC.
  • Always non-replenishable.
  • Always have the same cost.
  • Always have the same retail.
  • Supplier/Warehouse pack quantity is 1/1.
  • Items within the assortment can be added or removed.
