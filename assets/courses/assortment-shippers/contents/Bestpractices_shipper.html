Shipper - Best Practices (Cont'd)

Shipper Content Items

Below are some of the parameters to be considered while creating the content item numbers:
  • Replenishable or non-replenishable.
  • Same or different cost.
  • Same or different retails.
  • The content items are created in Online Item File.
  • Quantities are controlled by the buyer.
  • Must have active item status.
  • Must have the same 6-digit supplier number and 2-digit department.
