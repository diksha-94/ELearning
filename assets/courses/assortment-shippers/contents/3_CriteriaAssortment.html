Criteria for choosing an Assortment

The following must be considered while choosing an Assortment:

  • Cost and retail for all child item numbers have to be the same.
  • The UPC numbers must be different for each child item.
  • No replenishable items exist under the same UPC as the children.
    • Exception : Changing from replenishable items to an assortment.
  • When Parent is replenished, the entire assortment will be replenishable.
  • Quantity amounts are uncontrolled by the buying office.
    • The quantity of each child within the assortment is not specified.
    • The pack of the parent number controls the total number of pieces within the assortment that will be shipped.
  • On-hands and on-orders will roll to the parent item number.

If all of the above apply, then the merchandising associate may utilize an assortment.
