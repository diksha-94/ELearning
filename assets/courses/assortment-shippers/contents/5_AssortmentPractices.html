Assortment - Best Practices

Assortment Parent

Below are some of the factors to be considered while creating the assortment parent item number:
  • The Parent item number is created using a valid UPC number provided by the supplier (this barcode must not be on any product sold at the store).
  • Item type can be replenishable or non-replenishable.
  • Total pack quantity of assortment.
    • If 12 items will be shipped in the assortment, the pack for the parent item will be 12/12. Can be a breakpack parent.
  • Total cost of the assortment.
    • If a combination of the 12 items will be shipped and the individual cost on each child item is 1.00, the cost on the parent will be 12.00.
  • Unit retail will be the retail of one child item.
    • If the child item's retail is 2.78, the retail on the parent item will also be 2.78.
