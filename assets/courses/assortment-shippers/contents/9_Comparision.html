Assortment vs Shipper

Assortment Shipper
Assortment - Items in box are interchangeable and do not ship on their own - ever! Shipper - Items in box have defined quantites which are shipped every time, but often ship on their own
Each child in an Assortment will:
  • Have a different UPC Number
  • Have the same Cost, Retail & Pack
  • Be a non-replenishable Item
 Each content item in a Shipper will:
  • Have a different UPC Number
  • Can be a Different Cost, Retail and/or Pack
  • Can be the Same Cost, Retail and/or Pack
Parent must be created as follows:
  • In Online Item File - Create Assortment
  • Replenishable or Non Replenishable
  • Pack size: total quantity of assortment
 Prime must be created as follows:
  • In Online Item File - Create Shipper
  • Non Replenishable Only
  • Pack size: Could be break pack or case pack
Total Cost of Assortment (same unit cost as a child item) Total Cost of the Shipper (for case and unit)
Unit Retail of one Child Item Total Retail of the Shipper
Each child must meet below criteria:
  • Non Replenishable only
  • Supplier Pack = 1; Warehouse Pack = 1
 Each content item must meet below criteria:
  • Replenishable or Non Replenishable
  • Pack quantity as normal
Reporting & Visibility:
  • Assortment Parent holds on-hands, on-orders, & sales for all child items
  • Parent/Children viewed in Assortment Inquiry
 Reporting & Visibility:
  • Content Items hold individual on-hands, on-orders, & sales
  • Prime/Content items viewed in Shipper Inquiry
