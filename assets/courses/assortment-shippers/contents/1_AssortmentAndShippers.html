Assortment and Shipper

  • Assortments are commonly called "like assortments/items" as all of the items within a box are the same except for a specific detail such as color or size. All items within the assortment have the same cost and retail.

    • ASSORTMENT

  • Shippers are commonly called "unlike assortments/items" as the contents can either be completely different items combined in a box or similar items that need definite quantities of each.

    • SHIPPER
