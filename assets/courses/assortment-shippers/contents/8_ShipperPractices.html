Shipper - Best Practices

Shipper Prime

Below are some of the parameters to be considered while creating a shipper prime item number:
  • Always non-replenishable.
  • Total shipper cost.
  • Total shipper retail.
  • The prime is created in the "Create Shipper" link in online item file.
  • The prime item number is created using a valid UPC number provided by the supplier (this barcode must not be on any product sold at the store)
  • A 4000 bar code cannot be used by the supplier and must have a valid UPC number.
