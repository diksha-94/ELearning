Criteria for choosing a Shipper

The following criteria must be considered while choosing a shipper:

  • The UPC numbers must be different for each content item.
  • Replenishable items will be used to build the shipper or share the same UPC numbers as the content items.
  • Cost and retail is the same or different.
  • Buying office wants to track sales on individual content items.
  • The content items will be attached to the prime item on the purchase order only.
  • The entire shipper will be used for new modular set, PDQ, or special feature.
  • Quantity amounts are controlled by the buying office.
  • The quantity of each content item within the shipper is specified upon creation.
  • The supplier pack and warehouse pack quantity do not control the number of content pieces shipped.

If all of the above apply, then the merchandising associate may utilize a shipper.
